How To Play 😊

Normal Mode

The Pig Game is a fun and simple dice game for two players. The goal of the game is to be the first player to reach 100 points. Here’s how it works:


  • Players take turns to roll a single die.
  • In each turn, a player rolls the die as many times as they wish. Each result gets added to their turn total.
  • But be careful! If a player rolls a 1, their turn total is lost, and it becomes the other player’s turn.
  • At any time during a player’s turn, they can choose to “hold”, which means their turn total is added to their score, and it becomes the other player’s turn.
  • The first player to reach or exceed 100 points is the winner.

Remember, strategy plays a big part in this game. You must decide when to keep rolling and when to hold and pass the turn to your opponent. Good luck and have fun playing the Pig Game!


Hardcore Mode

In hardcore mode, the game is the same as normal mode, but with a twist. If a player rolls a 1, they lose all their points and it becomes the other player’s turn. The first player to reach or exceed 100 points is the winner.
